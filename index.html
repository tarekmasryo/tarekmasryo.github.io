About Me

Production-ready ML & GenAI systems, shipped end to end

Hi, I’m Tarek — an AI/ML Engineer who ships reliable ML and GenAI systems from data to deployment. I blend research-informed methods with practical engineering to turn ideas into decision-grade, measurable products.

  • End-to-end systems: data pipelines + modeling → deployable APIs + monitoring
  • Reliability by design: schema-validated outputs, typed interfaces, and observability to catch regressions early
  • GenAI/RAG when it adds measurable value: hybrid retrieval, reranking, context pruning, and evaluation telemetry to keep outputs grounded
  • Engineering rigor: operating thresholds, acceptance criteria, and latency/cost trade-offs that hold up in production
  • Currently exploring: orchestration and model routing for high-trust AI workflows.
  • Public work: reproducible projects and datasets across GitHub, Kaggle, and Hugging Face.
